About Us

At S R Refrigeration and Electricals, we have been providing top-notch AC maintenance and service solutions for over 17 years. With our expertise and dedication, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry, serving the community of Delhi-NCR and surrounding areas. We have a authorized service center of Bluestar.

As a company committed to customer satisfaction, we understand the importance of a well-functioning air conditioning system. Whether it's a residential or commercial space, we have the knowledge and experience to handle all your AC maintenance and service needs. Our team of skilled technicians is equipped with the latest tools and techniques to deliver efficient and reliable solutions.

At S R Refrigeration and Electricals, we are passionate about delivering reliable AC maintenance and service solutions. Trust us to keep your air conditioning system in optimal condition, providing you with comfort and peace of mind.

  • Regular Maintenance Visits: We schedule periodic visits to your premises as per the terms of the contract. Our expert technicians will thoroughly inspect, clean, and service your AC unit, ensuring it operates at peak performance.
  • Priority Service: As an AMC customer, you receive priority attention for any AC-related issues. In case of breakdowns or emergencies, our team will be at your doorstep promptly, minimizing downtime and discomfort.
  • Comprehensive Check-ups: During each visit, our technicians conduct a comprehensive inspection of your AC system. We assess the cooling efficiency, check refrigerant levels, clean filters, lubricate components, and perform necessary adjustments to optimize performance.
  • Proactive Repairs: Our skilled technicians are trained to identify and address potential issues before they escalate into major problems. By conducting regular maintenance, we can detect and resolve minor glitches, thereby preventing costly repairs in the future.
  • Cost Savings: With our AMC services, you can avoid unexpected repair expenses and minimize energy wastage. Well-maintained air conditioners operate efficiently, resulting in lower energy bills and enhanced durability.
  • Expert Advice: Our technicians are available to provide expert advice and guidance regarding the optimal usage and care of your AC unit. We are dedicated to ensuring that you have a clear understanding of how to maximize the performance and longevity of your system.

Our Services

Types of HVACs we provide services for :

Split AC

Our Split AC servicing is designed to ensure that your air conditioning unit operates at its peak performance, providing you with optimal cooling and energy efficiency. Our skilled technicians follow a comprehensive servicing process to address various aspects of your Split AC system.

Window AC

Our Window AC servicing is designed to ensure that your unit operates efficiently, providing you with optimal cooling and comfort. Our skilled technicians follow a comprehensive servicing process to address various aspects of your Window AC system.

Ductable AC

We specialize in maintaining and servicing ductable air conditioning systems to ensure optimal performance and comfort. Our comprehensive Ductable AC servicing includes a thorough inspection and maintenance of both the indoor and outdoor components of your system. Our skilled technicians follow a systematic approach to deliver efficient and reliable servicing.

Cassette AC

Our Cassette AC servicing is designed to optimize the performance and efficiency of your cooling system, ensuring a comfortable environment in residential and commercial spaces. Our skilled technicians follow a meticulous servicing process to address various aspects of your Cassette AC unit.

Ductless Mini Splits

Regular servicing of your Ductable Mini Split AC system helps maintain its performance, extend its lifespan, and prevent costly repairs. It also ensures efficient cooling and enhances indoor air quality, providing a comfortable environment for you and your occupants.

VRF Units

Our VRF unit servicing is designed to ensure optimal performance, energy efficiency, and longevity of your system. Our team of skilled technicians is well-versed in the intricacies of VRF technology and follows a comprehensive servicing process to address various aspects of your VRF system.

The services we offer are mentioned below :

AMC services & Maintenance

We go the extra mile to cater to all your air conditioning needs. In addition to our AC maintenance and repair services, we also provide comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) services to ensure the long-term performance and efficiency of your AC system.

Installation

When it's time for a new air conditioning system, we offer professional installation services. Our team will assess your specific requirements, recommend the most suitable AC unit, and ensure seamless installation for optimal performance and energy efficiency.

Servicing

Regular servicing is essential to prolonging the lifespan of your AC unit and maximizing its efficiency. Our comprehensive servicing includes cleaning, lubrication, inspection, and adjustment of components to keep your system operating at its best.

AC Repair

If your air conditioner is experiencing any problems, our experts will diagnose the issue and provide prompt and effective repairs. We strive to minimize downtime and restore your AC to optimal functioning as quickly as possible.

AC spare parts

Our inventory includes authentic spare parts for various brands and models of air conditioners. From compressors and fan motors to control boards and capacitors, we have the necessary components to restore your AC system to optimal performance. In addition to air conditioner spare parts, we also keep a selection of electrical accessories that complement your cooling system.

Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to ensuring your AC system operates at its peak performance. Our team will perform thorough inspections, clean the filters, check refrigerant levels, and identify any potential issues to keep your AC running smoothly.
